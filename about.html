About Rulindo Agro Marketing

1. About Rulindo District

Location: Rulindo District is located in the Northern Province of Rwanda.

Climate: It has a cool climate with sufficient rainfall.

Main Crops: Potatoes, beans, maize, cassava, tea.


2. Cooperative Mission & Vision

Mission: To promote sustainable agriculture and trade.

Vision: To become a strong and reliable agricultural cooperative.

  • Patrick - Tea - 12 years experience

4. Rulindo Map

summary

Rulindo District is one of the most productive agricultural regions in Rwanda. It benefits from fertile soils and favorable climate conditions that support various crops including potatoes, maize, and beans.

The cooperative plays an important role in connecting farmers to markets, improving income, and promoting modern farming techniques.
